# 📊 Progress Tracker

A comprehensive web-based progress tracker for documenting your innovation journey. This tool is designed for students in the MS Advanced Innovation program to track their goals, accomplishments, pivot points, and evidence of their work.

## Features

### 🎯 Core Functionality
- **Timeline View**: Visualize your journey with past work and future goals on an interactive timeline
- **Multiple Entry Types**:
  - 🎯 Goals - Document what you hope to achieve
  - ✅ Accomplished Tasks - Record what you've completed
  - 🔄 Pivot Points - Note challenges and how you solved them
  - 📸 Visual Evidence - Upload photos of work-in-progress and prototypes
  - 🔗 Resource Links - Save tutorials, articles, and helpful resources

### 📊 Weekly Summary
- Automatic calculation of weekly stats
- Visual cards showing:
  - Goals Set
  - Tasks Completed
  - Pivot Points encountered
  - Evidence pieces collected

### 💾 Data Management
- **Local Storage**: All entries are saved automatically to your browser
- **Export**: Download your data as a JSON backup file
- **Import**: Restore data from a previous backup
- **Clear**: Remove all entries (with confirmation)

### 📱 Responsive Design
- Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- Beautiful gradient interface with smooth animations
- Easy-to-use form with intuitive controls

## How to Use

### Adding an Entry
1. Select a **Date** for when you worked on or plan to do something
2. Choose an **Entry Type** from the dropdown (Goal, Task, Pivot, Evidence, or Resource)
3. Add a **Title** - a brief description of what you're documenting
4. Write **Details** - elaborate on your entry with more information
5. For Visual Evidence: Upload an image of your work
6. For Resources: Paste a link to a helpful tutorial or article
7. Click **Add Entry** to save

### Understanding Your Timeline
- **Blue dots**: Past entries
- **Yellow dots**: Future entries (goals/due dates)
- **Pink dot**: Today's date (marked with extra emphasis)
- **Entries alternate** left and right for visual clarity

### Weekly Updates
As recommended, update your tracker at least once per week with:
- What you accomplished
- Challenges you faced and how you solved them
- New goals for the coming week
- Photos of your progress

## Data Storage

Your entries are stored in your browser's **local storage**. This means:
- ✅ Your data persists between sessions
- ✅ No server required - fully private
- ✅ All entries stay on your device
- ⚠️ Clearing browser data will delete your entries

**Tip**: Regularly export your data to have secure backups!

## Backup & Recovery

### Exporting Your Data
1. Scroll to the "Data Management" section
2. Click "📥 Export Data (JSON)"
3. Your browser will download a backup file
4. Save this file in a safe location

### Importing Your Data
1. Click "📤 Import Data"
2. Select a previously exported backup file
3. Your data will be restored

## Tips for Success

💡 **Pro Tips**:
- **Document Frequently**: Don't wait until Friday to document. Take quick photos every time you work
- **Be Specific**: Use clear, descriptive titles so you can remember what each entry is about
- **Include Details**: Use the description field to explain your thinking and decisions
- **Visual Record**: Photos of prototypes, sketches, and work-in-progress are invaluable
- **Stay Organized**: Keep links to all resources you use in your tracker

## Keyboard Shortcuts
- Enter your date and start typing to quickly add entries
- Use Tab to move between form fields

## Browser Compatibility
- Chrome/Chromium (v90+)
- Firefox (v88+)
- Safari (v14+)
- Edge (v90+)

## Privacy
All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers. You have complete privacy and control over your information.

## Deployment

3. Select "Deploy from a branch"
